CodeRED F15

On April 3rd, 2015 CodeRED took Houston by storm. Hundreds of people from all over the world gathered for 24 hours to hack, snack, learn, have fun, and build something awesome. We're excited to announce we'll be doing it all again, bigger and better, in true Texas spirit; CodeRED Fall 2015.

So, prepare for a weekend filled with learning new technologies, pushing your limits, making new friends, solving problems, building out ideas, and redefining of excellence. We'll provide you with everything you need. Just make sure you come hungry and eager to build something awesome.

FAQ

Who can attend?

Any undergraduate and graduate student from any college, university, or higher education institution. Graduates who graduated within 1 year of the event may also attend.

Can high schoolers attend?

High schoolers may only attend if and only if they are 18 and/or can provide proof of enrollment in an institution of higher education.

How much does it cost?

Absolutely nothing for the event. We'll be providing snacks and meals, and sponsors will be handing out swag and prizes.

What do I need to bring?

A computer (preferably laptop), student ID, chargers, a sleeping bag if needed, and anything you need to maintain hygene.

How do I get there?

Drive, carpool, Uber/Lyft, etc. We'll have busses high demand locations.

How big can a team be?

No more than five people, please don't be one man team. The real fun and productivity happens when you're a team.

What if I don't have a team or idea?

Don't worry! That's pretty common, you'll have time before the hacking begins to find a team. We'll also have Facebook group for attendees.

What if I don't know how to code?

No problem! Designers and creatives are very common at hackathons. Want to learn how to code? We can help you out with that too.
